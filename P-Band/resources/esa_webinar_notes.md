# ESA Webinar Notes

## Webinar: "ESA P-band SAR Mission Status One Year After Launch"

---

### Key Takeaways

1. **BIOMASS launched April 2025** -- the first P-band SAR satellite in space
2. **P-band wavelength (~70 cm)** penetrates forest canopy to measure trunks and ground
3. **Primary mission:** Global forest biomass mapping leading to carbon stock estimation
4. **Secondary applications:**
   - Subsurface imaging in deserts (paleochannels, archaeology)
   - Ice sheet internal structure mapping
   - Topographic mapping via polarimetric interferometry
5. **Data products available:**
   - Level 1: Calibrated SAR images (backscatter)
   - Level 2: Forest height, Above Ground Biomass (AGB), forest disturbance maps

---

### Mission Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Frequency | 435 MHz (P-band) |
| Wavelength | ~70 cm |
| Orbit altitude | ~666 km |
| Spatial resolution | 50-200 m (depending on product) |
| Revisit time | 25 days |
| Mission duration | 5 years (minimum) |
